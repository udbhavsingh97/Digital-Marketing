Project Overview:

Consider that you are working at Guvi or any other reputed company in the role of a Digital Marketing Intern or Digital Marketing Trainee.
In this project, you will delve into the fundamentals of digital marketing.
These tasks will learn how to design and build a compelling web presence by understanding the inner workings of websites, including key ingredients and essential design principles.
Through hands-on activities and real-time exercises, you will create a website or landing pages from scratch, applying the knowledge of digital marketing strategies and best practices in website design.
